# Introduction

Anchor retrieval finds section-level signals before expanding local context.
Hybrid dense and sparse search improves recall on keyword-heavy queries.

## Method

The method is defined as anchor-first retrieval with tree expansion.
Parent-child hierarchy preserves document structure during chunking.

## Evaluation

Benchmarks compare Plain RAG, hierarchical chunking, and full AnchorRAG.
Metrics include recall at k, MRR, and nDCG at 10.
